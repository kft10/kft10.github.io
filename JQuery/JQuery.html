Chocolate-Mint
Chocolate Cookies

For 24 or more cookies.

Melted creme de menthe candies placed on top of small chocolate cookies. A delight for those who love mint and chocolate and perfect for gatherings!

This recipe makes 2 - 1/2 Dozen (depending on size of dough).

The bake time is 8-10 minuites at 350°F.

No experience necessary.

Ingredients

  • Semisweet chocolate chips: 1 cup (6 oz.)
  • Butter: 6 tablespoons
  • Light brown sugar: 3/4 cup
  • Water: 1 tablespoon
  • Egg(s): 1, large
  • All-purpose Flour: 1-1/4 cup
  • Salt: 1/2 teaspoon
  • Baking Soda: 1/2 teaspoon
  • Créme de menthe chocolate candies: 1 (4.67 oz.) package

Chocolate-Mint Chocolate Cookies

See Ingredients

Mix

In a small saucepan over medium heat, melt chocolatre chips and butter. In a large bowl with an electric mixer on medium-low speed, combine the melted chocolate mixture, brown sugar, and water; let cool slightly. Slowly beat in an egg. Gradually add flour, salt, and baking soda untill well combined. Cover and put the mixture into the refrigorator. Let the mixture chill for 1 hour.

After Refrigeration

Pre-heat oven to 350 degrees F. Shape dough into 1-inch balls and place 2 inches apart on greased cookie sheets.

Bake

Bake 10-8 minuites or untill the edges are set and the middles are soft and slightly puffy. Remove from oven and palce on a cooling rack.

Cut 15 candies in half and place them a half on top of each warm cookie; let sit for 1 minute. Gently spread warmed chocolate over tops of cookies and let cool slightly. Chop remaining candies and sprinkle on top of cookies, as shown in the photo.


Enjoy!
